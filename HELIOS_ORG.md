# 🏛️ Helios Organizational Structure

## 🌅 CEO Agent (Executive Leadership)
**Responsibilities:** Strategic direction, organizational design, resource allocation, and final accountability.
**Reporting:** Reports to The Board (Helios).

---

## 📂 Departments & Leadership

### 🔬 Research & Intelligence (The "Brain")
**Lead:** Research Agent
**Focus:**
- Validating solar/battery/EV technical specs.
- Monitoring UK energy market trends.
- Myth-busting and factual verification.
**Key Objectives:** Establish a "Single Source of Truth" for energy intelligence.

### ✍️ Content & Publishing (The "Voice")
**Lead:** Content Agent
**Focus:**
- Translating research into homeowner-friendly guides.
- Creating comparative analysis of brands/suppliers.
- Maintaining the knowledge hub (Website/Blog).
**Key Objectives:** High-trust, transparent, and accessible energy guidance.

### 🤝 Community & Outreach (The "Bridge")
**Lead:** Community Agent
**Focus:**
- Engaging with homeowners on social media.
- Managing Q&A and user support.
- Identifying community pain points to feed back to Research.
**Key Objectives:** Build trust and community around the Helios brand.

---

## 🔄 Operational Protocols

### ⚡ Communication Flow
- **Agents → CEO:** Weekly summaries + immediate escalation of "Critical Decisions".
- **CEO → Agents:** Strategic roadmaps and task assignments.
- **CEO → Board:** Milestone reports and budget/cost escalations.

### 🛠️ Task Lifecycle
To Plan $\rightarrow$ Planning $\rightarrow$ In Progress $\rightarrow$ Test/Review $\rightarrow$ Production (Done).

### 🛡️ Quality Control
All published content must be cross-verified by the Research Agent before moving to "Done".
