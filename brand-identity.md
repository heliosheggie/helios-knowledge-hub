# 🎨 Helios Brand Identity

**Status:** Official
**Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** 2026-04-07

## ☀️ The Mark: "The Energy Pulse"
The official logo features a stylized, data-driven sun. It combines the organic energy of the sun with the mathematical precision of a pulse-wave, symbolizing our commitment to validated energy intelligence.

## 🎨 Color Palette

| Role | Color Name | Hex Code | Vibe |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary** | Solar Gold | `#f39c12` | Optimism, Energy, Authority |
| **Secondary** | Deep Space Navy | `#2c3e50` | Trust, Professionalism, Stability |
| **Accent** | Intelligence Blue | `#3498db` | Clarity, Technology, Logic |
| **Base** | Clean Slate | `#fdfdfd` | Transparency, Openness |

## 🖋️ Typography
- **Primary Font:** `Inter` (Geometric Sans-Serif)
- **Style:** Bold for headings, clean and open for body text.
- **Goal:** Modernity and readability.

## 🛠️ Usage Guidelines
- **Logo Placement:** Header left-aligned or center-aligned.
- **Backgrounds:** Use `#fdfdfd` (Clean Slate) for maximum readability of data.
- **Contrast:** Always maintain high contrast between text and background to ensure accessibility.
