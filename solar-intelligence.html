Solar Intelligence Engine

Personalised solar potential for your exact property.

Home → Solar Intelligence Engine

Purpose

The Solar Intelligence Engine gives homeowners trusted, personalised solar insights using real scientific data.

Components

  • Historical irradiance
  • 7–14 day solar forecast
  • Seasonal yield expectations
  • Cloud cover + temperature impact
  • Shading estimation (optional)

Data Sources

  • Open‑Meteo Solar API
  • NASA POWER
  • PVGIS (EU Commission)
  • Met Office (limited free tier)
